Frequently Asked Questions

What is DeFi yield farming?

DeFi yield farming involves lending or staking cryptocurrency assets to earn rewards, typically in the form of additional tokens or interest payments.

Simply connect your wallet, choose your preferred assets, and start earning yields from our curated selection of 20+ protocols.

Is my investment safe?

We only feature audited protocols with strong security track records. However, all DeFi investments carry inherent risks.

What are the fees?
